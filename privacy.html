隐私政策

Privacy Policy

欢迎使用 Lightning Renamer。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策说明了我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

Welcome to Lightning Renamer. We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. 数据收集与处理

1. Data Collection and Processing

Lightning Renamer 遵循极其严格的“隐私优先”（Privacy-by-Design）原则。我们不收集、不处理且不存储任何个人数据。具体而言：

Lightning Renamer strictly abides by the Privacy-by-Design philosophy. We do not collect, process, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • 完全本地化处理：您的所有文件读取、重命名与写入操作均严格受限在您的本地设备上执行，绝不存在将任何文件或文件名上传至云端的行为。
  • 100% Local Processing: All file reading, renaming, and writing operations run securely constrained within your local device. Under no circumstances are files or filenames uploaded to cloud servers.
  • 无遥测或诊断数据：我们不收集您的设备指纹、操作系统信息、应用崩溃日志或使用习惯统计。
  • Zero Telemetry: We do not log or collect device fingerprints, OS information, crash reports, or usage analytics.

2. 信息共享与披露

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

鉴于本应用程序从运行机制上不收集任何数据，因此在逻辑上与法律上，我们均无法、也绝不会向任何第三方网络、广告商、分析机构或执法部门出售、交易或披露您的信息。

Because the application structurally avoids collecting any user information, it is both logically and legally impossible for us to sell, trade, or otherwise disclose data to third-party networks, advertisers, analytics entities, or law enforcement.

3. 数据安全性

3. Data Security

Lightning Renamer 严格遵守 macOS 的 Sandbox（沙盒）安全隔离机制。这意味着应用程序仅能在您明确授权访问的文件夹权限作用域内运行，彻底杜绝了程序针对系统进行未授权扫描或篡改文件系统的风险。由于无外部数据离线，客观上从根源上消除了我方服务器端产生数据泄露（Data Breach）的可能。

Lightning Renamer operates under macOS Sandbox restrictions. The app can only interact with directories you explicitly grant access to, firmly mitigating risks of unauthorized filesystem scanning or tampering. Given the offline nature of our architecture, the risk of a server-side data breach on our end is fundamentally non-existent.

4. 政策更新

4. Policy Updates

随着应用功能的演进或相关法律法规的变化，我们保留对本隐私政策进行更新和修订的权利。当我们对其作出重大实质性修改时，更新后的版本将会连同“最后更新”日期一起公布于此页面中。

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our application or relevant legal frameworks. When material revisions are made, the updated version will be posted on this page along with the effective "Last Updated" date.
